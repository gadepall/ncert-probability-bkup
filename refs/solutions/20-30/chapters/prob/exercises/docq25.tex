Let $X_1,X_2 \in \cbrak{1,2,3,4,5,6}$ represent the two dice.
\begin{align}
\pr{X_1\ne X_2} = \frac{6\times5}{6\times 6}= \frac{5}{6}
\end{align}
Then,
\begin{multline}
\pr{X_1+X_2 = 4| X_1\ne X_2} 
\\
= \frac{\pr{X_1+X_2 = 4, X_1\ne X_2}}{\pr{X_1\ne X_2}}
\\
= \frac{\frac{2}{36}}{\frac{5}{6}}
=\frac{1}{15}
\end{multline}
