Let $X \in \cbrak{0,1}$ such that 0 represents black.  Then, 
\begin{align}
\pr{X = 0} = \frac{x}{12}
\end{align}
%
If 6 more black balls are put in the bag, 
\begin{align}
\pr{X = 0} = \frac{x+6}{12+6}
\end{align}
From the given information
\begin{align}
 \frac{x+6}{12+6}
&= \frac{2x}{12}
\\
\implies x &= 3
\end{align}
