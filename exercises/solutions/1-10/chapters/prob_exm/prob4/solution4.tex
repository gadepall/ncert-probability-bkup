Let $X \in \cbrak{i}_{i=1}^{6}$ and $f_i$ be the correspnding frequency.  Then, 
\begin{align}
\pr{X=i} &= \frac{f_i}{1000}
\end{align}
and
\begin{align} 
\pr{X=6} &= \frac{14}{200}
\\
&= 0.07
\end{align}
