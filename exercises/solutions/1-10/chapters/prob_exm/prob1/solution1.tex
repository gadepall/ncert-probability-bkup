Let $X\in\cbrak{0,1}$ represent the random variable, where $0$ represents head and 1 represents tail.  From the given information,
% given that $\to$
%\\
%No of experiment = 1000
%\\
%No of heads = 455
%\\
%No of tails = 545
%\\
%probability of comming heads = P$X=0$
%\\
\begin{align}
\pr{X=0} &= \frac{455}{1000}
\\
&= 0.45
\\
\pr{X=1} &= 1-\pr{X=0}
\\
&= 0.545
\end{align}
