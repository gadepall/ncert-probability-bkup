From the given information,
\begin{align}
\pr{X=0} = 70 {\%} = 0.7\\
\pr{X=1} = 30 {\%} =0.3
\end{align}
Hence,
\begin{align}
    E(X) &= 1\times0.7 + 0\times0.3 =0.7
    \\
    E(X^2) &= 1^2\times0.7 + 0^2\times0.3 =0.7
    \\
    \implies Var(X) &= E(X^2) -[E(X)]^2
    \\&= 0.7- 0.7^2 = 0.21
\end{align}
