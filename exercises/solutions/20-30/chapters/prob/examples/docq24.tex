From the given information, using the fact that $A,B$ are independent,
\begin{align}
\pr{A+B} &= \pr{A}+\pr{B}-\pr{AB}
\\
&= \pr{A} + \pr{B-AB} 
\\
&= \pr{A} + \pr{A'B}
\\
&= \pr{A} + \pr{A'}\pr{B}
\\
&= \pr{A} + \pr{A'}\brak{1-\pr{B'}}
\\
&= \pr{A} + \pr{A'}-\pr{A'}\pr{B'}
\\
&=1 - \pr{A'}\pr{B'}
\end{align}
