\begin{align}
l &= (9-6)kms\\
  &= 3kms 
\end{align}
\begin{align}
w&=(4.5-2)kms\\
 &=2.5kms
\end{align}
As the lake is rectangular;
\begin{align}
a &={l}\times{w}\\
&={3kms}\times{2.5kms}\\
&=7.5sq.kms
\end{align}
$$L=9kms$$
$$W=4.5kms$$
The whole region is of rectangular shape. Hence the area of the whole region is;
\begin{align}
A &={L}\times{W}\\
&={9kms}\times{4.5kms}\\
&=40.5sq.kms
\end{align}
